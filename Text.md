# Visual Identity & Style

- **Theme:** Modern, minimalist, and premium fashion aesthetic inspired by high-end streetwear brands.
- **Layout:** A clean, card-based grid system with generous white space to allow product imagery to stand out.
- **Components:** Rounded corners (approx. 12–16px) on all cards and buttons to create a soft, contemporary feel. Subtle, soft shadows are used to provide depth without cluttering the UI.
- **Navbar:** A sophisticated glassmorphic navigation bar that stays fixed or translucent, featuring clear iconography for Search, Wishlist, and Cart.

# Typography & Color

- **Typography:** Bold, high-impact sans-serif fonts for primary headings (e.g., "New Drop: Oversized Collection") and clean, legible medium-weight fonts for product titles and descriptions.
- **Color Palette:** A neutral, high-contrast palette focusing on whites, light grays, and deep blacks to emphasize the colorful product photography.

# Section Structure

- **Hero Section:** Full-width high-resolution banners with centered or left-aligned bold typography and dual CTA buttons (Shop Now / Explore).
- **Category Grid:** Interactive cards with background images and title overlays for quick navigation to specific collections like Anime, Marvel, or Streetwear.
- **Product Cards:** Clean layouts showing the product image, title, price, and a quick-action "Add to Cart" or "Wishlist" icon.
- **Promotional Banners:** High-contrast sections designed to break the grid and highlight seasonal sales or specific collection drops.
